AuraFit
Live Demo: AuraFit

AuraFit is a modern fitness platform that provides users with personalized workout plans, structured nutrition guidance, and progress tracking.

Features
Home – Overview of AuraFit’s offerings

Hero Section – Engaging landing page with key highlights

Membership – Subscription and membership details

Classes – List of fitness programs available

Shop – Store for fitness-related products

Pricing – Plan details and cost breakdown

Playlist – Curated workout music and video content

Navbar & Footer – Easy navigation across the site

Contact – Support and inquiries
